How it works

Three steps to a live website

01
We talk on call

Share your idea, a rough brief, or even just what your business does. Our team asks the right questions — type of business, pages you need, anything you like from other sites. No forms to fill, no long wait times.

02
We build, you give feedback

We send you a working preview — usually within 3–5 days. You review it on your phone or laptop, we do one round of revisions together. You don't need to know any technical terms.

03
Your site goes live

Our team handles domain setup, SSL, hosting configuration — or hands off files to your existing host if you have one. Most sites are fully live within a week of our first conversation.

Common questions

Do we need to provide the content?

Basic content (your services, contact details, a few photos) helps, but our team can help structure and write copy if you're not sure what to say.

What if we already have a domain?

We can deploy directly to your existing domain and host. If you don't have one yet, our team will recommend a cheap, reliable setup.

Will we be able to make edits later?

Yes. We hand over all files at the end. For simple text changes, our team can teach you how to do it yourself in under 10 minutes.

What's the payment process?

50% upfront, 50% on delivery. UPI or bank transfer. No payment until we've agreed on the scope.
